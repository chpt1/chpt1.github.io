Welcome to my personal blog!

Hey! What’s up everyone? My name is Tae and earlier this summer, I decided to start a blog. Why? In the past few years, I picked up a lot of new interests such as being involved with the tech community and programming. It seemed to be a waste not to log all of the things that I’ve been doing. Thus, I am planning on blogging some of the cool events that I attend such as hackathons and conferences. Also, as someone who is very interested in the startup culture, I am going to try to learn a lot more about them by interviewing startups of all types and stages and blogging them on here.  

I will do my best to blog at least once a week. In the beginning, it’ll be mostly personal blogs and start-up company interviews.  

Questions? Interested in being part of my blog? Please email me at taehong.min18@outlook.com or tweet me @taehongmin1. I’ll get back to you as soon as possible.
