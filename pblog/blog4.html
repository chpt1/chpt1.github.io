2018 Challenges and Bucket List:

 

In light of 2018, I’m going to attempt my very best complete challenges I put on for myself and cross off a couple things off my bucket list while I’m at it. 2017 flew by way too quickly without being able to accomplish many things or challenging myself anywhere near to my fullest potential.  Some of it will be very simple things that everyone else may just shrug it off as something mundane but to me will be a big deal.

 

No more excuses. No more complaining. I’m just going to do it.

 

2018 started off really well, actually - err, I guess technically not because I started off with catching a pretty nasty cold from being out on New Year’s Eve. I had a Full Marathon scheduled at Disney World in Orlando, FL on the 7th. I was supposed to do it with a friend but due to the snowstorm that crashed the east coast on the 4th, his first (along with many others) got cancelled and was not able to make the marathon. It was something I was looking forward to since signing up for it in July 2017 but due to being sick, I did not have the courage nor the motivation I did months ago.

 

But I suck it up and decided to just do it anyway.

 

On January 7th, 2018, I completed my my first 26.2 miles at the Disney World Full Marathon.

 

Now, it’s time to conquer more challenges.
