Terry Piercon’s Year in Review:

There’s a very good chance no one will read this so I’m going to just go reflect this year without any filters:

 

2017 was one of the most action packed, eventful, and dramatic moments of my life. It was definitely nowhere near the best year of my life but certainly a lot of things happened. Keep in mind I graduated undergrad in the Spring of 2016. Let’s dig into it chronologically:

 

January

  • Switched from United to Delta. I have not flown United Airlines since.
  • Switched to the SPG Hotel chain. The only other hotel chain I’ve been to was a Marriot which is part of the same company due to their merger (or acquisition) but I’ve only been to a Marriot maybe once or twice.
  • Installed Uber for the first time.
  • Moved to New York City for the first time in my life in the Hell’s Kitchen borough to a studio.
  • Flew the first time in my life to Vista, California. There was barely anyone there.
  • Learned Django Rest Framework.
  • Gained 15 lbs.

February

  • Flew every week Monday to Friday to Vista, California. Population did not increase.
  • Flew to Charlotte, NC for a weekend for the first time.
  • Upgraded to Silver status on Delta with fast track.
  • Upgraded to Silver status on SPG with fast track.
  • Downloaded Tinder. Deleted it immediately afterwards upon realization that it wasn’t an app to help you build a fire in the wild.
  • Downloaded Coffee meets bagel. Deleted it immediately due to similar reason above.
  • NYC Subways decided to increase their train delays by 10x. The F train is nearly nonexistent.
  • Started coding in Node.js and postgresql.
  • Made a friend in NYC.
  • Gained 6 lbs.

March

  • Continued to fly every week Monday to Friday to Vista, California. Population did not increase.
  • Flew to Clinton, Michigan for a weekend for the first time.
  • Upgraded to Gold status on Delta with fast track.
  • Upgraded to Gold status on SPG with fast track.
  • Upgraded to VIP Platinum status on Uber due to riding with uber 10-15 times a week.
  • Signed up for the Lincoln Tunnel 5K. At this point, I was not even able to jog 1 mile without losing my breath and my sides aching.
  • First sign of my dogs barking.
  • Got late to a first date in West Village due to train delay on the A and got slapped across the face upon meeting. Rest of the date was pretty good. Let’s call her Marchacha.
  • Started coding in Ruby on Rails.
  • Lost the friend I made in NYC.
  • Lost 2 lbs.

April

  • Continued to fly every week Monday to Friday to Vista, California. Population did not increase.
  • Upgraded to Platinum status on Delta with fast track. Upgraded to Delta Comfort every time now.
  • Upgraded to Platinum status on SPG with fast track. Upgraded to suite rooms every time now.
  • Still VIP Platinum status on Uber due to riding with uber 10-15 times a week.
  • Completed my Lincoln Tunnel 5K!!!
  • My dogs continued to bark.
  • Marchacha and I are in a relationship and we see each other every weekend when I fly back.
  • Decided to resume my passion in cooking and cooked for Marchacha every weekend.
  • Signed up for a half marathon in Brooklyn in June.
  • Started coding in Scala.
  • My lost friend wants to be found…?
  • Lost 5 lbs.

May

  • Continued to fly every week Monday to Friday to Vista, California.
  • Flew first class first time due to Delta Platinum upgrade.
  • Marchacha and I flew to Tyler, TX for a weekend for the first time.
  • Marchacha moved into my studio. She doesn't like when my dogs bark.
  • Attended TechCrunch Disrupt NY Hackathon and built InstaGrammy, which is Instagram but for grandmothers. Tech stack: iOS (Swift & OBJ-C, DRF, Postgresql, Clarify).
  • No new friends but my lost friend is now found.
  • Lost 6 lbs.

June

  • Continued to fly every week Monday to Friday to Vista, California.
  • Upgraded to first class on every flight and hotel suites every time now.
  • Marchacha wanted to get married soon so I proposed to Marchacha. We became engaged and set our engagement party and wedding date.
  • Completed my half marathon in 9:15 min per mile average pace!!!
  • My dogs continued to bark but used to it at this point.
  • Started crossing language training with Russian and Spanish. Hola. Как дела?
  • Found friend is now my friend again.
  • Lost 3 lbs.

July

  • Continued to fly every week Monday to Friday to Vista, California.
  • Took out a loan for $15,000 and got her a replacement diamond ring from Tiffany’s as requested.
  • Engagement party in a private resort area in Disneyland, FL. I forgot to tell my parents but facetimed them. They were not pleased.
  • Started to use Docker for all of my apps.
  • Flew to Waterbury, Connecticut for the first time.
  • Gained 6 lbs

August

  • Continued to fly every week Monday to Friday to Vista, California. I was really getting tired of flying from NY to Cali every week at this point. I was always anxious and mentally tired.
  • Marchacha wanted to have a wedding in Paris in October and fly everyone out. I was losing money at this point and so decided to take out another loan for this despite my parents telling me this was a bad idea.
  • Threw her a surprise birthday party at Le Bain and got her the new pairs of boots she wanted but was scolded for not getting her the matching winter coat.
  • Friend enlisted in the marines upon realizing, he didn’t want to be a cashier intern at rite aid anymore.
  • Gained 7 lbs.

September

  • Continued to fly every week Monday to Friday to Vista, California.
  • Marchacha forgot my birthday so I got chipotle and watched The Office.
  • Friend dropped out from the Marines to continue my InstaGrammy idea.
  • My dogs barked a bit less now.
  • Lost 2 lbs.

October

  • Continued to fly every week Monday to Friday to Vista, California.
  • Came back early one Friday to surprise her the weekend before our wedding.
  • Caught her in our bed with another man.
  • Marchacha is leaving me for a man named Estaban. He wears the same tank top every day.
  • Cancelled wedding plans and Marchacha left my studio.
  • My dogs are silent.
  • Gained 12 lbs.

November

  • Continued to fly every week Monday to Friday to Vista, California.
  • Every weekend went to a different city: Rochester, Minnesota; Norman, Oklahoma; Olathe, Kansas; Cancun, Mexico.
  • Went to the gym 6 times a week.
  • Can speak fluently in Russian and Spanish.
  • My dogs began to bark again.
  • Lost 15 lbs..

December

  • Did not travel at all.
  • Started volunteering at a local church to teach kids how to program in Java.
  • Started dating a girl who’s half Russian and half Colombian.
  • Paid for my sister’s winter tuition.
  • Took my parents out to steak dinner in NYC to thank them for everything.
  • Dogs are barking normally now.
  • Realized it’s not about losing weight but staying fit, healthy, and happy.
